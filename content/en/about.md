## Hi, I'm Ed Jaramillo

I'm a **Pricing Strategy Analyst** at Teradata, based in Tijuana, Mexico. I work on competitive pricing, TCO modeling, and helping enterprise customers understand the value of data platforms.

I'm also an **AI builder** — not by training, but by obsession. I run a self-hosted AI agent that helps me manage finances, track tasks, research competitors, and automate my workflows. I built it on a 10-year-old laptop turned into a Linux server.

### What I write about

- **Pricing & Competitive Strategy** — TCO models, win/loss analysis, how cloud platforms actually price
- **AI Agents & Automation** — building, deploying, and taming self-hosted AI
- **Data & Analytics** — Power BI, SQL, DAX, and making data useful for decision-makers
- **Personal Systems** — how AI changes how you manage money, time, and attention

### Languages

This blog is bilingual. I write in English and Spanish. Some posts are in both languages, some in one — depending on the audience.
